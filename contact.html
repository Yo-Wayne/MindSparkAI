Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our AI education program

How can I bring this program to my school?

Contact us using the form above or email us directly. We'll schedule a call to discuss your school's needs, available technology, and how we can adapt our curriculum to fit your schedule and classroom environment.

Is there any cost for schools?

No, our program is completely free for all schools. We believe AI literacy should be accessible to every student, regardless of their school's budget. All materials, training, and support are provided at no cost.

What technology requirements do we need?

Students need access to computers or tablets with internet connectivity. Most AI tools we use are web-based and work on standard devices. We can also help arrange equipment donations if needed.

How can students get involved as volunteers?

High school and university students can help deliver workshops, assist younger students with AI tools, create educational materials, and document program impact. It's a great way to gain leadership experience and build your portfolio.

What age groups do you serve?

Our curriculum is designed for middle and high school students (ages 12-18), but we can adapt materials for different age groups based on your school's needs and student readiness.
